Our Story

Nurturing quality from the soil to your plate.

The Foundation

Rooted in Agricultural Excellence

Aryal Multipurpose Agricultural Farm Pvt. Ltd. was established with a singular vision: to elevate the standard of local dairy production in Nepal. Located in Dhunibesi-01, Dhading, our farm benefits from the rich natural resources and pristine environment of the Bagmati Province.

We recognized a gap in the supply chain for high-performance dairy products, particularly mozzarella cheese, tailored for the demanding needs of commercial kitchens. By combining traditional dairy wisdom with modern hygienic processing, we have positioned ourselves as a premier supplier for restaurants, cafes, and hotels who refuse to compromise on quality.

Our Mission

To deliver impeccably hygienic, premium-grade dairy products that empower local culinary businesses to create extraordinary food experiences with consistent supply.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred agricultural dairy partner in Nepal, known for sustainable practices and uncompromising product excellence.
